Meet Our Security Team

Our Security Guards

Duties Allocation Behaviour

  • A Guard must be neat always in or out of duty.
  • A guard must observe total punctuality.
  • A guard must maintain a high degree of discipline while in and out of duty.
  • A guard should not smoke or take any alcoholic drugs or any other related narcotic drug.
  • A guard must always observe and maintain total honesty.
  • Absenteeism without permission is a serious offence and punishable according to our constitution.
  • A guard should not move from the place of duty assigned without duty permission from his/her senior.
  • A guard must be vigilant and attentive, always staying aware of their surroundings and ready to respond to any suspicious activities or emergencies promptly.
  • A guard must adhere to all safety protocols and procedures, ensuring a secure environment for both themselves and those they are protecting.

Training

  • Our guards are well trained and disciplined.
  • Our guards are courageous, efficient and dedicated to their duty.
  • We have supervisors during day and night to monitor the activity of the guards on assignments.
  • Our reputation precedes as a company that sets benchmarks for its competitors.
  • Our security personnel undergo rigorous training programs to ensure they are equipped to handle any situation.
  • Our guards are vigilant, proactive, and committed to maintaining a safe environment for all clients.
  • We conduct regular performance evaluations to ensure our team meets the highest standards of excellence.
  • We prioritize continuous improvement, regularly updating our protocols to adapt to emerging threats.
