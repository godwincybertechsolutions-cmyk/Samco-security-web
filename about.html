COMPANY HISTORY

"Our company began operations in November 2019 and is operational 24/7. Our security guards promptly report any incidents or observations that are unusual or of significant concern to the person in charge. They maintain detailed records of vehicles entering the premises, including the time of arrival and departure. While on duty, our security guards are always fully dressed in the company uniform. Additionally, they undergo regular training to ensure they are equipped to handle any situation effectively. Our commitment to safety and security remains our top priority."
